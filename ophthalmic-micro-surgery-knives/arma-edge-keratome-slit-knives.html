Arma Edge® Keratome (Slit) Knives

Arma Edge® Keratome (Slit) Knives: Designed for sclerocorneal incisions in cataract surgery. Available in various sizes including Regular and Clear Cornea to match the chosen Phaco tip and surgical access needs.

The Acucut Slit (Keratome) knives are ophthalmic surgical instruments utilized in various ophthalmic procedures, including cataract surgeries. The ultra-sharp slit blade tips with a flawless cutting edge facilitate superior wound architecture and minimal wound leakage.
